FAQ

What's the format?

Everyone rolls out together and we all ride centuries!
That's 100 kilometers, 100 miles, or 100 nautical miles.

What should I bring?

  • A working bike
  • Tools for mechanical issues & the requisite knowledge
  • Water
  • Fully charged lights (just in case)
  • The route

Is there SAG support?

Nope! See above

Is this a race?

Nope! Bike at comfortable pace, take stops, meet new people!

What's a nautical mile?

The length of 1/60th of a degree around the equator
(approximately 1.15 miles)

What's a kilometer?

1/10,000th of the distance from the equator to either pole
(approximately 0.62 miles)

Is the earth round?

Yes
